Pumpkin Juice

Description

In the Harry Potter books, pumpkin juice is mentioned in all but the first book in the series as one of the most popular wizarding beverages (the other being butterbeer, of course). It is served in the Great Hall at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and on the Hogwarts Express snack trolley. This is one magical treat that you are going to love!

Start a new tradition this year by making a batch of pumpkin juice to enjoy with some popcorn and watch the Harry Potter Movies. Or better yet, re-read the books!

Ingredients

  • Canned/Homemade Pumpkin Puree
  • Apple Cider or Apple Juice
  • Apricot,Pear or Peach Nectar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Pumpkin Pie Spices

Steps

  1. Stir all of the ingredients together in a large pot on the stove
  2. Let it simmer for a bit to allow the flavors to blend
  3. Serve it warm or cold
